Mysore self-practice

At Do Yoga London we teach yoga in a traditional method known as ‘Mysore self-practice’, which originated im Mysore, South India. Students are taught individually within a group environment and practise at their own pace with one-to- one advice and sometimes adjustments give by the teacher.

Working in this way allows you to focus on your breath and encaurages a meditativ qualty to your practice. Complete newbies are always welcome - join us for more information on how to get started and what to expact.
